Brimstone

is a controller agent in VALORANT, known for his leadership and tactical abilities. He is the first agent of the VALORANT Protocol and serves as its commander. His abilities include deploying smokescreens to block vision, boosting allies' fire rate and movement speed, and using an orbital arsenal to provide strategic advantages during matches. Brimstone's real name is Liam Byrne, and he has a background as a firefighter and soldier. He is designed to support his team and enable effective site executions
